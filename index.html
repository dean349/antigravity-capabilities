🧠 Loaded Skills

Skills are specialized instructional frameworks tailored for marketing, sales, growth, and conversion rate optimization (CRO).

1. Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) & Product Flows

  • ab-test-setup: Plan A/B and multivariate tests to measure if changes improve conversions.
  • churn-prevention: Design offboarding exit flows, save offers, and "failed payment" recovery sequences.
  • form-cro: Optimize non-signup forms to reduce friction.
  • onboarding-cro: Re-engineer the post-signup "first run" experience to activate users faster.
  • page-cro: Audit and optimize standard marketing pages specifically to increase signups.
  • paywall-upgrade-cro: Optimize in-app prompts and upsell screens to convert free users to paid.
  • popup-cro: Design conversion-focused exit-intent popups and notification banners.
  • signup-flow-cro: Streamline account creation flows to minimize abandoned signups.

2. Copywriting & Content Creation

  • ad-creative: Generate and iterate bulk ad copy for Facebook, Google, and LinkedIn.
  • competitor-alternatives: Craft targeted "Us vs. Them" comparison pages.
  • content-strategy: Plan high-level blog topics and editorial calendars.
  • copy-editing: Review and tighten up existing text.
  • copywriting: Write persuasive net-new marketing copy for websites and calls-to-action.
  • social-content: Ideate and format content for organic social platforms.

3. SEO & Site Architecture

  • ai-seo: Get your product cited in AI-generated answers (ChatGPT, Perplexity).
  • programmatic-seo: Strategize the creation of template-driven pages targeting specific data-points.
  • schema-markup: Write JSON-LD structured data for rich snippets in search results.
  • seo-audit: Diagnose technical SEO issues and Core Web Vitals.
  • site-architecture: Map out website hierarchy and internal linking strategies.

4. Marketing Strategy & Operations

  • analytics-tracking: Establish Google Analytics (GA4) and event tracking.
  • free-tool-strategy: Plan "engineering-as-marketing" campaigns to generate leads.
  • launch-strategy: Prepare go-to-market plans for product launches.
  • marketing-ideas: Brainstorm creative growth tactics.
  • marketing-psychology: Apply cognitive biases and behavioral science to strategy.
  • paid-ads: Advise on strategy, targeting, and ROAS for paid media campaigns.
  • pricing-strategy: Help determine plan pricing and packaging tiers.
  • product-marketing-context: Establish your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) and positioning.
  • referral-program: Design affiliate systems and viral loops.

5. Sales & Email Outreach

  • cold-email: Write B2B cold outreach campaigns designed to get replies.
  • email-sequence: Map out automated lifecycle/drip emails for your database.
  • revops: Optimize CRM data hygiene and the marketing-to-sales pipeline handoff.
  • sales-enablement: Create assets for sales reps (pitch decks, demo scripts).

6. AI Automation & UI Tooling

  • nlm-skill: Guides automation workflows using the NotebookLM CLI.
  • notebooklm-mcp-skill: Teaches the agent how to link prompts to the notebooklm MCP tools.
  • ui-ux-pro-max: A massive design-intelligence repository with 50 styles to ensure code looks premium.

🛠️ How to Use Skills

You do not need to type any special commands or complex prompts. The system is designed to automatically detect when a skill is needed.

1. Just Ask Naturally (Recommended)

Simply describe your goal, and the most relevant skill will automatically activate.

  • "Can you review the pricing page on my website and tell me why it isn't converting?" ➡️ Triggers page-cro and pricing-strategy.
  • "I need to write a 4-part welcome email series for new signups." ➡️ Triggers email-sequence.

2. Explicitly Request a Skill

If you want to ensure a specific framework is used, mention the skill by name.

  • "Please use the seo-audit skill to review my website."
Starting a new marketing project?

Begin by saying: "Let's run the product-marketing-context skill." This establishes a foundational profile of your product and target audience, ensuring all subsequent marketing tasks are perfectly aligned with your brand positioning!
